1. Foundation & Site Preparation

Before ordering your Hardcore and Marrum, ensure the site is excavated to the solid 'red soil' or rock layer. For a standard 3-bedroom house, you will require approximately 7,000 to 8,000 stones. Use Marrum for backfilling to ensure a stable base that does not sink during the rainy season.

2. Selecting the Right Stones

9x9 Dressed Stones: These are machine-cut for precision. They require less mortar (cement/sand mix) and provide a faster building speed. Ideal for external walls where a neat finish is required.

6x9 Stones: Best used for internal partitioning walls. They are lighter and help reduce the overall load on the foundation while maximizing internal floor space.

3. Material Quality Standards

Always check for "ringing" sound when two stones are struck together; this indicates high density and strength. Our Ballast is crushed to 20mm, perfect for high-strength concrete lintels and floor slabs.

4. Quantity Management

To avoid project delays, always order in batches of 1,000 stones. This allows for better site space management and easier offloading for the delivery trucks.
